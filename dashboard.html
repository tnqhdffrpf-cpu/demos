Three.js r184: the great "stop allocating per frame" pass
The March release (r184) cleaned up a long-standing source of jank: Three.js had been allocating hundreds of thousands of transient objects per second in hot paths. r184 eliminates most of those per-frame allocations, and complex scenes now hold frame-time much better. Combined with r171's WebGPU-by-default (including Safari 26 shipping WebGPU on macOS/iOS/visionOS), it's finally "just works" across the board.
